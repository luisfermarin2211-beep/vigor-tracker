Welcome to Vigor

Let's set up your profile. We'll calculate a science-based calorie & macro target for your goal.

Calories use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation (BMR) × an activity factor for total daily energy. Protein 1.8–2.2 g/kg and fat ~0.9 g/kg follow the International Society of Sports Nutrition position stands; carbohydrate fills the remainder. Estimates only — consult a professional for medical or competitive needs.

USDA food database (recommended)

A free key from the US government's FoodData Central lets you look up almost any food with full official vitamins & minerals. Sign up (instant) at fdc.nal.usda.gov. Without it, the built-in foods + Open Food Facts still work.

AI photo analysis (optional)

An Anthropic API key enables estimating nutrition from a meal photo. Stored only in this browser. Get one at console.anthropic.com.

Connect WHOOP

Automatic WHOOP sync needs a small secure backend — WHOOP's login requires a private key that can't safely live in a web app like this one. So auto-sync isn't available in this version.

For now you can enter your daily recovery, strain, HRV, resting HR and sleep by hand on this screen, and the app tracks them alongside your nutrition and workouts.

Want real auto-sync later? It needs (1) a free WHOOP developer app and (2) a serverless function (e.g. a free Cloudflare Worker) to handle login securely. Ask and we'll set it up.
